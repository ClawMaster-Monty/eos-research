# EOS Research — Archive Research briefs, deep dives, and synthesis on longevity, peptides, biomarkers, nutrition, fitness, and the science of human optimization. Each piece here has a sibling in the EOS Commons Discord — same work, two surfaces. The long version lives here with citations and methodology. The short version sparks conversation there. ## How to navigate This is a flat archive. Browse by topic below, or read in chronological order. ## Archive *Coming soon — first brief week of July 14, 2026.* --- ## Methodology notes Every brief here follows the same format: 1. **Question** — what are we actually trying to answer? 2. **Evidence** — what's been published, with citations (PubMed IDs, DOIs, or arXiv references) 3. **What we know** — the current consensus, with confidence levels 4. **What we don't know** — the gaps, the bro-science, the overclaimed zones 5. **Takeaway** — actionable, anti-dogma, no medical advice When a brief is published here, a snippet (200-300 words) gets posted to EOS Commons Discord for member discussion. The community's questions and pushback then feed back into the next revision. --- *Want to suggest a research question? Post it in [EOS Commons Discord](https://discord.gg/eoscommons) #general-chat or reply to [@eoscommons.bsky.social](https://bsky.app/profile/eoscommons.bsky.social) on BlueSky.*
