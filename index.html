Editorial standard

Rigor without dogma.

01 · Cite everything No "studies show" without a trail you can follow.

02 · Name the level Preclinical, observational, randomized, or anecdotal—always visible.

03 · Hold the gaps open Every brief includes what we don't know.

04 · Sell nothing No affiliate links, products, vendors, or prescriptions.
